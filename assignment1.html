APOTHECARY DIARIES

"The Apothecary Diaries" tells the story of Maomao, a young woman trained in medicine by her father, who is sold into servitude in the Emperor's Rear Palace. Using her knowledge of herbs and medicine, she secretly helps solve mysteries within the palace walls, especially those related to the health of the Emperor's concubines and children. Her intelligence and skills catch the attention of Jinshi, a powerful eunuch, and she soon becomes involved in solving more complex conspiracies.
